Partha Ghosh

My research interests are in Computer Vision and Robotics, specifically, 3D reconstruction, neural rendering, language-vision, scene understanding and parsing, with applications in autonomous driving, robotics and augmented reality.

I completed my Master's degree in Machine Learning from the University of Tübingen and Bachelor's degree in Mathematics from the Indian Statistical Institute.

Currently, I am working as a Machine Learning Engineer at DKFZ (German Cancer Research Center). Prior to that, I worked as a Machine Learning Engineer at Aurivus. I also worked as a Research Assistant at the Autonomous Vision Group developing a research paper recommender system.
